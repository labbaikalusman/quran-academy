Activities

We Offer

Noorani Qaida

Build a strong foundation in Quranic reading with our Noorani Qaida course. Designed for beginners, this program focuses on Arabic alphabets, pronunciation, and basic Tajweed rules, preparing students for fluent Quran recitation.

Quran with Tajweed

Master the art of Quranic recitation with proper Tajweed. Our expert instructors guide you through each verse, ensuring accurate pronunciation and rhythm, fostering a deeper connection with every word of the Holy Quran.

Quran Memorization

Embark on a spiritual journey to memorize the Holy Quran with personalized guidance from qualified instructors. Our structured program ensures steady progress, helping students retain and perfect each verse with Tajweed and understanding.

Tafseer ul Quran

Deepen your understanding of the Holy Quran through comprehensive Tafseer sessions. Explore the meanings, context, and wisdom behind each verse, guided by knowledgeable scholars who connect timeless revelations with everyday life.

Masnoon Du'aan

Learn the daily supplications (duas) taught by the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) for every occasion. Our program helps students memorize, understand, and incorporate these powerful prayers into their daily lives

Hadith Sharif

Discover the teachings and sayings of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) through authentic collections of Hadith. Our sessions focus on understanding the context and wisdom of each narration, helping students apply these timeless lessons to their daily lives.

Method of Prayers

Learn the correct method of performing Salah (prayers) with step-by-step guidance on postures, recitations, and supplications. Our instructors ensure students understand the physical and spiritual aspects of prayer

6 Kalimah

Equip yourself with the foundational declarations of faith in Islam. Our program helps students memorize and understand the meanings of the 6 Kalimah, strengthening their belief and connection with core Islamic principles.

Method of Abulution

Master the proper steps of performing Wudu (ablution) with accurate guidance on each action and its significance. Our lessons ensure students understand the spiritual and physical purification process required before prayers and worship.
